Jeff's Hockey Career

Early Years

Jeff has been playing hockey since he was 6 years old, and continues to play to this day. He started out playing Roller Hockey on the streets, and then started to play Ice Hockey on recreational teams. Fast forward a few years, and Jeff was playing on multiple Roller Hockey and Ice Hockey teams. He was playing 4-5 times a week, and multiple times a day.

High School Years

Jeff's high school did not have a team, so he was able to play for another school in the league and played High School Ice Hockey for Carey-West Hempstead Freshman through Junior Year. He also was play High School Roller Hockey Sophmore through Senior Year for another neighboring school. His senior year, he played Ice Hockey for the NY Apple Core travel organization.

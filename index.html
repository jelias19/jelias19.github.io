Jeff's Hockey Career

Early Years

Jeff has been playing hockey since he was 6 years old, and continues to play to this day. He started out playing Roller Hockey on the streets, and then started to play Ice Hockey on recreational teams. Fast forward a few years, and Jeff was playing on multiple Roller Hockey and Ice Hockey teams. He was playing 4-5 times a week, and multiple times a day.

High School Years

Jeff's high school did not have a team, so he was able to play for another school. Freshman year he played for Valley Stream and then played High School Ice Hockey for Carey-West Hempstead Sophmore through Junior Year. He also played High School Roller Hockey Sophmore through Senior Year for another neighboring school. His senior year, he played Ice Hockey for the NY Apple Core travel organization.

College Years

Jeff attended the University at Buffalo for both undergraduate and graduate studies. He played 5 years of Roller Hockey in the NCRHA for the school team, where they won 2 Regional Championships and attended Nationals 5 years.
